Spyder Events

SPYDER 2026

National Level Student Technical Symposium with the Title “SPYDER” will be conducted in the Information Technology department every year to encourage the UG and PG students to come up with technical ideas. The Symposium will feature a mix of technical and non - technical events. We seek to bring together practitioners to consider the latest thinking in our sector and to hear from those who are applying and researching the latest thinking in technical services.

Paper Presentation

Topics:
The Technical Symposium invites original research contributions under the following thematic tracks(but not limited to):
Artificial Intelligence, Medical intelligence System, Bio Inspired Intelligence, Ethical and Trustworthy AI, Generative AI Systems, Quantum AI Systems, Intelligent Pattern Mining, Cognitive Networks & AI
Rules:
  1. Max 2 members per team.
  2. PPT must not exceed 10 slides.
  3. Use clear readable font and minimal text.
  4. Presentation Time : 5 - 7 Minutes.
  5. Paper must be original and unpublished.
  6. Abstract should not exceed 100 words.
  7. Full paper should not exceed 1500 words.
  8. Paper should be submitted Ms-Word format.
  9. Please mail your abstract, paper and PPT to the respective Email - itspyder.hicas@gmail.com before 07.03.2026.

Ideathon

Rules:
  1. Max 2 members per team.
  2. Participants should present their idea with a ppt and a prototype(if available).
  3. Copying of idea is strictly prohibited.
  4. judging will be based on innovation and feasibility of the idea.
  5. Please mail your idea, prototype and PPT to the respective mail ID - itspyder.hicas@gmail.com before 07.03.2026.
  6. Decisions taken by judges remains final.

Debugging

Rules:
  1. A team should consist of maximum 2 members.
  2. In all rounds question have to be answered in stipulated time.
  3. Exploring the internet will not be permitted.
  4. Electronic gadgets will not be entertained.
  5. Any kind of unfair & mis-behaviour will lead to disqualification.

Web Designing

Rules:
  1. Max 2 members per team.
  2. Students have to design a web-site on any one of the specified topics and present it to the judges.
  3. First page must be your Homepage (Containing LOGO/picture depicting the topic and the website name).
  4. Three pages must be dedicated to your discussion suitable for the website.
  5. Total timing -2 hrs.
  6. The last page must be Information Hub that provides details.
  7. Use of Open source tools are disqualified.
  8. Decisions taken by judges remain final.

Poster Designing

Rules:
  1. Max 2 members per team.
  2. On the spot topic will be given for poster designing.
  3. Total timing -2 hrs.
  4. Participants can use any tools like Adobe Photoshop, CorelDRAW & MS-Paint.
  5. Participants are not permitted to use online logo makers copyrighted resources if found they will be disqualified.
  6. The poster will be judged according the following criteria :creativity and presentation and relevance to the theme.
  7. Decisions taken by judges remain final.

Biz Masters

Rules:
  1. Teams must consist of 2-5 members
  2. All Biz Masters strategies or ideas must be developed during the event and must be original.
  3. Teams must align their Ideas with the provided theme.
  4. Duration: 3-5 minutes presentation.
  5. Stuffs will be judged based on creativity, feasibility, alignment with the theme, and presentation quality.
  6. Decision taken by judges will be final.
